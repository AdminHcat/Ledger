### Ledger Chat Themes

This is a list of preset themes for your Ledger messages. Copy/paste the block into your `color` section to use

```toml
# Colors in hex format
primary = "#009688"
primaryVariant = "#52c7b8"
secondary = "#1e88e5"
secondaryVariant = "#6ab7ff"
```
